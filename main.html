Objective

The primary objective of house planning is to design a functional and comfortable living space by efficiently utilizing the available area, considering the needs of the occupants, while also incorporating aesthetic appeal, all while adhering to budget constraints and local building regulations; essentially, to create a well-organized layout that meets the specific requirements of the homeowner within the given site limitations. A significant goal of planning is to reduce the likelihood of errors. A well-thought-out plan reduces the likelihood of errors and oversights. Although mistakes and oversights cannot be completely avoided in any field, we can reduce them to some extent through proper planning.
